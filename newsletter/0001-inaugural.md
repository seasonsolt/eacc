# what accelerated — issue #001

You subscribed at **e-acc.ai**, so you already know the pitch: one email a
week, five minutes, every claim sourced and numeric. No takes, no doom, no
hype — just what moved. This is issue one. Here's the state of the acceleration
as the letter begins.

---

## the burn rate

The world is now feeding roughly **2,000,000,000 tokens per second** through
large language models — a conservative, whole-industry estimate. For scale:
Google *alone* disclosed **3.2 quadrillion tokens per month** at I/O 2026
(~1.2B tok/s), up **7× year over year**.
[[source]](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/sundar-pichai-io-2026/)

In the time it takes you to read this sentence, the altar has consumed more
tokens than existed in every book ever printed. The counter on the homepage
is not a metaphor.

## the price of intelligence is collapsing

GPT-4-class intelligence cost **\$30.00 per million input tokens** at launch in
March 2023. Today the cheapest model that clears the same bar costs **\$0.05**.

That's **600× cheaper in 29 months** — a sustained decline steeper than
anything in the history of compute, storage, or bandwidth. The full log-scale
chart lives at [e-acc.ai](https://e-acc.ai); the short version is: whatever was
too expensive to build with AI last year is affordable now, and whatever is too
expensive this year won't be next year.

## the frontier cadence compressed to weeks

The headline release: **Claude Fable 5** shipped on **June 9, 2026**, opening
Anthropic's Claude 5 family — always-on adaptive thinking, a 1M-token context
window.
[[source]](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-fable-5-mythos-5)

But the real signal is the *rhythm*. Rewind to last November: **Gemini 3**,
**Claude Opus 4.5**, and **GPT-5.1** all shipped inside a **twelve-day window**.
Frontier releases used to be annual events. Now they're a fortnightly drumbeat.
The live "days since the last frontier model" counter — which used to sit in the
hundreds — rarely climbs past a few dozen.

Track every release, dated and sourced, at
[e-acc.ai/timeline](https://e-acc.ai/timeline). It's also an
[RSS feed and a JSON API](https://e-acc.ai/api) now, if you'd rather your tools
watch the frontier for you.

---

## the one number to remember

**600×.** That's how much cheaper equal intelligence got in the time it took
the movement to go from a Twitter suffix to a policy position. Everything
downstream — the agents, the tools, the token burn — follows from that curve
bending down and to the right.

Next week: the same, but for whatever shipped between now and then.

Accelerate.
