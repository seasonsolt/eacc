# e-acc.ai is an audience asset, not a product launchpad

The domain earns ~2k organic visits/month on the "e/acc" cultural term. After a
grilling session (2026-07-05) weighing "monetize the traffic" against "use it to
launch an AI-infrastructure product" (a BYOK cost-routing gateway was the strongest
candidate), the owner chose **audience asset**: zero-backend content pages that
capture search intent and convert to the newsletter, monetized in this order —
newsletter audience → sponsorship/affiliate → ads on long-tail pages only after
six-figure monthly UV. The homepage stays ad-free permanently.

Consequences: the calculator and pricing pages are *SEO capture surfaces feeding the
funnel*, not products — "this feature is unimpressive as a product" is not a valid
criticism of them. Product-scale ideas (gateways, observability, routing) are out of
scope unless this ADR is explicitly superseded; they compete with funded incumbents
(OpenRouter et al.) and require infrastructure, custody of user keys, and full-time
commitment that contradict the one-person weekly-update operating model.
